Essential Skills Overview

  • Grip: Use either shakehand or penhold. Ensure fingers are relaxed and the paddle feels like an extension of the hand.
  • Forehand: Rotate your waist and use your whole body to generate power. Follow through in the direction you want the ball to go.
  • Backhand: Keep your elbow in front of your body, brush the ball with control, and shift weight slightly forward.
  • Serve: Toss the ball vertically and hit from behind the baseline. Mix spin and placement for strategic advantage.
  • Backspin / Chop: Slice under the ball with a downward motion. Often used defensively to force errors.

Match Rules Summary

  • Matches are best of 5 or 7 games.
  • Each game is played to 11 points, with at least a 2-point lead to win.
  • Players alternate serves every 2 points, switching every game.
  • If the score reaches 10–10, serves alternate each point.
  • Players switch sides after each game and during the final game when one player reaches 5 points.
  • The ball must be thrown vertically at least 16cm when serving, and visible throughout the serve.
